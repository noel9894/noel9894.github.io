À propos de moi...

I am a civil engineering student at The Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur, India interested in anything that mostly involves coding and logic.


I am from Kerala, a state better known as ‘God’s Own Country’. I am proud that my home town Wayanad is one of the most beautiful places in one of the most ravishing states in the country.

My mother tongue is Malayalam, a beautiful yet complex language with a strong role in the history of the state and nation. Once someone told me it was the second hardest language to learn in the world!


I was born into a Christian Orthodox family, which helped me inculcate the virtues needed in life with more ease. Needless to say, I am grateful to my parents for helping me with that.
