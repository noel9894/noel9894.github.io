Interests...

My interests have always displayed a dynamic nature ever since I was a kid. So I’m not what to put in here. Well what say we talk about my present interests, huh?

  • Computers

    It has always been computers. These things practically amaze me. Although I have been using a system since I was 3 years old. I love fixing the issues that pop up while using the system; don’t misunderstand me for debugging codes for I am not much of a coder. I am a ‘trouble-shooter’ (I am not sure whether that word exists). Whatever the matter, if it’s even slightly related to computers, I seem to understand them more.


  • Games

    I also have a passion for games. Generally I prefer the action packed H&S sort of thing, but I may also go for sports-based high-end games like FIFA. Not a big fan of multiplayer gaming. My problem with these hypnotising hobbies is that I often lack the determination to stop playing. I tend to play for long hours in a single go, finishing of large games in short time. Don’t get me wrong, I am proud that I am a gamer, but I just wish I could enjoy them more.


  • Football

    I just love football. Before I got ‘seriously’ involved in acads I used to follow the Barclays and Spanish premiere leagues well. Now I am just content to playing the sport real-time. When I play I just give my total dedication to the game (It has gifted me generously with a lot of dislocated ankles and torn ligaments, luckily not a fracture till date). I fell the spirit of sports and competition all through those ninety minutes. I’d love to play again but a ligament tear in my ankle has put me on the benches.
